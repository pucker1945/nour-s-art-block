born on the 1st of june 2005, an aspiring syrian artist living in the UAE pushed through all stereotypes and cultural standards and faced so many harsh difficulties in her life in order to create what our hearts can feel but minds can't put into words. Her talents glow when it comes to pouring feelings and emotions into her art. Numerous paintings of her do not require a description nor explanation for they showcase exactly how the human heart feels. Whether its love, sorrow, derealization, or even joy, Nour Mansour was able to perfectly express each one of these emotions regardless of the impossibility of expressing them through words. Her art brings inspiration and glee throughout many of her fans whether its online or through the art galleries in which her hard work was presented. But the one thing that inspires the most is the love she has for the art itself, the peace and joy she feels while drawing is always projected through the way she speaks about the art.
